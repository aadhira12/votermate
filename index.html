Welcome to Votermate!

“The right to vote is the cornerstone of our democracy. It’s how we, the people, participate in the process of forming our more perfect union.” – Barack Obama

Votermate is a Web App designed to help citizens with the necessary tools to navigate the 2024 Election cycle. Whether this is your first time voting or you are a seasoned voter, this website is designed to support you in every step of the way.

The app is developed by Aadhira Satheesh – a middle school student at Acera School.
